About Us

Dirt Bikes USA was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Carbondale, Colorado. The company manufactures and sells its own brand of off-road motorcycles for riders who value performance, durability, and reliability.

  • Offers both enduro and motocross motorcycle models for different riding needs.
  • Known for quality components, customization, and product innovation.
  • Focused on customer satisfaction and strong off-road performance.

Dirt Bikes USA sells through a distributor network across the United States and also reaches some international customers. Its product line includes the Enduro 250, Enduro 550, Moto 300, and Moto 450, giving riders options for both cross-country and motocross racing.

Enduro 250

Enduro 550

A more powerful enduro model built for riders who need stronger performance on demanding off-road terrain.

Moto 300

A motocross bike made for speed, agility, and quick handling on enclosed dirt tracks and race-style riding conditions.

Moto 450

A high-performance motocross model designed for riders who want more power and a stronger competitive riding experience.
